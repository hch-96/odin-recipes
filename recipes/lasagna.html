Lasagna Recipe

Description

This classic lasagna is a comforting Italian dish made with layers of tender pasta sheets, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Egg
  • Salt and pepper
  • Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Boil the lasagna noodles until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, cook the ground beef with seasoning until browned.
  4. Add tomato sauce to the cooked beef and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with the egg, salt, and pepper.
  6. In a baking dish, layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella.
  7. Repeat the layers and finish with mozzarella and Parmesan on top.
  8. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake another 10 minutes.
  9. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving.
